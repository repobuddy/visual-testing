# Migrating to 3.0

[storybook-addon-vis][storybook-addon-vis] 3.0.0 supports Storybook 10.

This document will guide you through the process of migrating your project from Storybook 9 to Storybook 10.

## Migration Steps

1. Update your `package.json` to use the new version of [storybook-addon-vis][storybook-addon-vis].
2. Update your `vitest.config.ts` to use the new version of [storybook-addon-vis][storybook-addon-vis].
3. Update your `storybook.config.mjs` to use the new version of [storybook-addon-vis][storybook-addon-vis].
4. Update your `storybook.config.mjs` to use the new version of [storybook-addon-vis][storybook-addon-vis].
